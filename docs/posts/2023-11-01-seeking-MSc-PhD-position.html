This post collects some links useful in answers to emails about “Seeking PhD position” or “Prospective MSc student”.

The CSE department (shared between Chalmers and UGOT) offers education programmes at BSc, MSc, and PhD (doctoral) level. The admission to BSc and MSc programmes is handled nationally for all Swedish universities at http://www.universityadmissions.se/ and a good description of the application procedure is available on Chalmers central pages. The CSE information is available here.

The doctoral education is handled differently, and each PhD position is announced openly as an (academic) employment opportunity here

https://www.chalmers.se/en/about-chalmers/work-with-us/vacancies/

Pick “Computer Science and Engineering” as the department and “PhD Student Positions” as “profession”.

—-

If your interests match that of the Functional Programming Group, do get in touch, but please note that the formal application for education programmes or PhD positions is not handled “locally” by us.

Kind regards, Patrik Jansson
