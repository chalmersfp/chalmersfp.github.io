I am a Senior Lecturer (Universitetslektor) at the University of Gothenburg and Chalmers in Sweden. I am a member of the Functional Programming unit in the department of Computer Science and Engineering. I am the head of the bachelor Computer Science programme (Datavetenskapligt program). My research interest are functional programming languages, property-based testing, and how we can use software technology to improve education.
